About Us

The TraveLog is the destination for cataloging and planning all of your adventures anywhere in the world. Capture life as you live it, share your achievements with others, and plan for your next greatest moments.

TraveLog was founded by 4 friends who wanted to make the experience of sharing their adventures with each other better. With what started as a way to make their stories meaningful and fun, is now available for everyone to enjoy.

It is the joy of sharing these moments that inspire us every day to improve this experience. To make every passing moment as exciting as the ones up ahead.
